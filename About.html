Kevin Bubut, a music enthusiast with a nose for talent, curates the website. He spends her days combing through online forums and local gigs, searching for the next big thing. In 2010, Kevin Bubut, a young music blogger, inherited a box of dusty cassette tapes from her uncle. Filled with demos from unknown artists and forgotten bands, they sparked an idea. Asep, frustrated with the mainstream music scene, decided to create a platform for these hidden gems. With a basic website builder and a shoestring budget, Willfy was born.
